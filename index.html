Welcome to my little corner of the Internet! This website is currently a work in progress.

I'm currently a senior undergraduate physics and mathematics double major at the University of California, Berkeley. I am broadly interested in high-energy theory, especially with applications toward particle phenomenology.

In my undergraduate years I have been fortunate to work deeply on a variety of orthogonal projects. I am currently writing my senior thesis with Prof. Ori Ganor working on 6D (2,0) superconformal quantum field theories and the renormalizability of 'curvepole' field theories. I have also worked with Prof. Yury Kolomensky in experimental particle physics as part of the Mu2e experiment, in addition to a side project analyzing the claimed X17 particle. Finally, I worked with Prof. Mustapha Ishak (at UT Dallas) constraining cosmological parameters in Modified Gravity theories toward the dark energy problem.
